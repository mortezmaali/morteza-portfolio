About

Small bio

Morteza Maali Amiri is a color and imaging scientist with advanced training in Imaging Science and Color Science from Rochester Institute of Technology. His work has focused on problems such as color reproduction, spectral and colorimetric recovery, HDR imaging, image registration, virtual cleaning of artworks using deep learning, and characterization of multispectral imaging systems.

He currently works as a Color/Imaging Scientist through DigitalFish on contract with Meta, contributing to projects involving color perception and reproduction, image and video quality, AR/VR/MR color perception, skin tone reproduction, and automated capture workflows.
